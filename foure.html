Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone

Welcome to my favorite book webpage. This book is one of the most popular fantasy novels in the world.

About the Book

Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone is the first novel in the Harry Potter series.

It was written by J. K. Rowling and published in 1997.

The story follows a young boy who discovers that he is a wizard and begins his magical journey at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry.

About the Author

J. K. Rowling is a famous British author.

She became one of the world's most successful writers through the Harry Potter series.
